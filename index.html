About UCSS

Ukrainian Canadian Social Services - Ottawa Branch is a non-profit charitable organization serving the Ukrainian community in Ottawa. Guided by the needs of individuals and families of Ukrainian community, who experience language and cultural barriers. We provide a range of support services.

Поранених зустрічаємо.../ відвідуємо .../ психологічно працюємо.../випроваджуємо ...
